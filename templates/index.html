GPU Troubleshooting

No GPU detected or GPU acceleration is not enabled. Follow these steps to enable GPU acceleration:

  1. Update your NVIDIA drivers to the latest version from the NVIDIA website
  2. Install PyTorch with CUDA support using this command:
    pip install torch torchvision torchaudio --index-url https://download.pytorch.org/whl/cu118
  3. Check if CUDA is properly installed by running:
    nvidia-smi
  4. Restart your computer after installing or updating drivers

Even without GPU acceleration, Whispr will still work using your CPU, though transcription will be slower. For the best experience, we recommend using the "tiny" or "base" models for CPU-only mode.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Whispr

What is Whisper?

Whisper is a general-purpose speech recognition model developed by OpenAI. It's trained on a large dataset of diverse audio and is a multitasking model that can perform multilingual speech recognition, speech translation, and language identification. The model uses a Transformer sequence-to-sequence architecture trained on various speech processing tasks, allowing a single model to replace many stages of a traditional speech-processing pipeline. Whispr leverages this technology while running it entirely on your local machine, ensuring your audio data never leaves your computer.

Which model should I choose?

For most everyday use, we recommend starting with the turbo model, which is optimized from large-v3 for faster transcription with minimal accuracy degradation. If you need maximum speed and have limited resources, try the tiny or base models. The medium and large models deliver the highest accuracy but require significant GPU resources. Note that English-only models (.en) tend to perform better for English content, especially for the smaller models. Check the System Requirements section to determine which models are compatible with your hardware.

Is my data sent to any servers?

No, Whispr processes everything locally on your computer. Your audio and video files are never uploaded to any external servers for processing. The only thing that might be downloaded are the Whisper model files themselves (only the first time you use a specific model), which are then cached locally for future use. This ensures complete privacy and data security, making Whispr ideal for sensitive or confidential material.

What file formats are supported?

Whispr supports a wide range of audio and video formats including:

  • Audio formats: MP3, WAV, FLAC, OGG, M4A
  • Video formats: MP4, AVI, MOV

If you have a file in another format, you may need to convert it first using FFmpeg or an online converter. FFmpeg is also required by Whisper for audio processing. For optimal results, ensure your audio is clear and has minimal background noise.

How accurate is the transcription?

Whisper's performance varies widely depending on the language and model size. The accuracy depends on several factors:

  • Model size: Larger models (medium, large) offer higher accuracy
  • Language: Performance varies significantly across different languages
  • Audio quality: Clear audio with minimal background noise yields better results
  • Speaking style: Well-articulated speech is transcribed more accurately

English-only models (.en) tend to perform better for English content, especially for the tiny and base models. The large model can achieve excellent accuracy in ideal conditions, while smaller models trade off some accuracy for faster processing.
